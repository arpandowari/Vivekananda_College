Central Computer Lab

Keeping in view the growing importance of computer learning, the college has established a well-equipped computer lab to keep the students abreast with the latest computer knowledge. Our college has a very spacious air-conditioned Computer Laboratory with 33 computers. All the systems are equipped with the necessary software and packages, LCD projection systems, printers, scanners, and are up to date with the current technology and generation. All have internet connectivity. Students are allowed to avail the facility of this lab under the supervision of their departmental teachers.

Faculty and staff have access to the computer labs. The computers in the lab are well configured and suitable for the users. These computers are upgraded as per the need of university syllabus and the software version change. This lab is also available to carry out some computer-based training courses. At present, a course is running in collaboration with IIT, Bombay.
